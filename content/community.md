## Community Guidelines

This is an academic community. Please observe the following principles:

1. **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people. Constructive criticism is always welcome.
2. **Be precise.** When discussing mathematics, try to be as clear and rigorous as possible.
3. **Be generous.** Share references, alternative explanations, and context freely.
4. **Give credit.** Acknowledge others' contributions and cite sources.
5. **Stay on topic.** Discussions should relate to mathematics, machine learning, or the content on this site.
